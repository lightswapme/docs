LightSwap can work with partners on a revenue-sharing model for swaps created through partner-driven integrations.

This page explains the model at a high level. Exact commercial terms are handled directly with LightSwap.

## What fee sharing means

In general, fee sharing means a partner can receive a share of swap-driven revenue from eligible partner traffic or partner-created swaps.

LightSwap usually works with a partner fee share in the `0.5%` to `2%` range.

The exact percentage, payout cadence, and eligibility rules are agreed directly with LightSwap.
