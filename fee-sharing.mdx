LightSwap can support revenue sharing for eligible swaps created through an approved integration.

Exact commercial terms are handled directly with LightSwap.

## What fee sharing means

In general, fee sharing means your business can receive a share of swap-driven revenue from eligible integration traffic or API-created swaps.

LightSwap usually works with a fee share in the `0.5%` to `2%` range.

The exact percentage, payout cadence, and eligibility rules are agreed directly with LightSwap.
