**LightSwap** revenue sharing helps crypto apps, wallets, Web3 platforms, and affiliate users earn from swap activity connected to their integration or link.

Revenue sharing supports B2B widget embeds and direct API usage. Individual users can also share an affiliate link and earn from swaps made through that link.

## Integration

Integration revenue share is agreed directly with LightSwap and usually ranges from `0.1%` to `3%`.

The exact rate depends on the commercial agreement with LightSwap. It can cover swaps created through a widget embed or direct API usage.

## Affiliate links

Affiliate links let users share LightSwap and earn `0.2%` from swap volume made through their link.

**Example**:

1. `100` invited users make swaps.
2. Each user swaps `$1,000` on average.
3. A `0.2%` affiliate reward equals `$2` per swap, or `$200` total.

## Payouts

You can request revenue-sharing payouts from your [LightSwap dashboard](https://lightswap.me/dashboard).
